Shimla — Snow Paradise

Colonial charm, pine forests, snow-laden peaks, Mall Road vibes and breathtaking hill views — Shimla is the queen of Himachal.

Best time: Dec–Feb (snow) · Apr–Jun Ideal trip: 3–5 days Altitude: 2,206 m

Quick Overview

Shimla, the capital of Himachal Pradesh, is known for its scenic beauty, Victorian-style architecture, snowy winters, pine forests and romantic hilltop vibes. It is one of India’s favorite winter destinations.

Top Places to Visit

  • Mall Road & The Ridge
  • Kufri Snow Point
  • Jakhoo Temple & Hill
  • Kalka–Shimla Toy Train (UNESCO)
  • Green Valley Viewpoint
  • Christ Church
