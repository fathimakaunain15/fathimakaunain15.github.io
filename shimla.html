Shimla — The Queen of Hills

Colonial charm, snowfall views, Mall Road walks, Jakhu Temple sunsets & Kufri adventure. A beautiful premium guide with photos, itinerary & tips.

Himachal Pradesh Best time: Oct–Feb (snow) / Mar–June Altitude: 2200 m

Quick Overview

Shimla, known as the Queen of Hills, offers beautiful pine forests, snowy mountains, colonial architecture and charming local streets. Whether it's Mall Road walks or Kufri adventure sports, Shimla provides the perfect hill-station vibe.

Top Attractions

  • Mall Road & Ridge
  • Jakhu Temple
  • Kufri Snow Viewpoint
  • Christ Church
  • Green Valley & Mashobra

Travel Tips

  • Wear comfortable shoes for Mall Road walking.
  • Snowfall possible in winter—carry jackets.
  • Jakhu monkeys are naughty—keep things zipped.

3-Day Shimla Itinerary

Day 1 — The Ridge & Mall Road

Walk around The Ridge, Christ Church, and enjoy the colonial architecture. Evening shopping & food at Mall Road.

Day 2 — Jakhu Temple & Mashobra

Visit Jakhu Temple for panoramic views, then explore Mashobra & Green Valley.

Day 3 — Kufri Snow Adventure

Head to Kufri for snow activities, horse rides, and scenic photography.
