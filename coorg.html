Coorg – The Scotland of India

Coorg, officially known as Kodagu, is a beautiful hill station located in Karnataka. Surrounded by lush green coffee plantations, misty hills, and waterfalls, Coorg is popularly called the “Scotland of India”.

Why Visit Coorg?

Coorg is perfect for travelers who love nature, peace, and scenic landscapes. It offers fresh air, pleasant weather, and a calm atmosphere away from city life.

Best Time to Visit Coorg

The best time to visit Coorg is from October to March. The weather remains cool and ideal for sightseeing, trekking, and outdoor activities.

Top Places to Visit in Coorg

  • Abbey Falls: A beautiful waterfall surrounded by greenery
  • Raja’s Seat: Sunset viewpoint with panoramic views
  • Talakaveri: Origin of River Kaveri
  • Coffee Plantations: Guided plantation walks

Things to Do in Coorg

  • Nature walks and trekking
  • Visit coffee estates
  • River rafting in Barapole River
  • Photography

Culture & Food

Coorg has a rich culture influenced by the Kodava community. Popular local dishes include Pandi Curry, Akki Rotti, and traditional coffee.

Travel Tips

  • Carry light jackets during evenings
  • Book stays in advance during peak season
  • Respect local traditions and nature

Is Coorg Worth Visiting?

Yes, Coorg is one of the most peaceful and scenic destinations in South India. It is ideal for couples, families, and solo travelers seeking relaxation in nature.
