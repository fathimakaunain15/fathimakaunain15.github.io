Coorg — Forests, Coffee & Waterfalls

Misty mountains, coffee plantations, wildlife, rafting & peaceful viewpoints.

Karnataka Best Time: Oct – May Coffee • Waterfalls • Trekking

Quick Overview

Coorg (Kodagu) is called the Scotland of India. Known for its rolling hills, dense forests, coffee estates, waterfalls and adventure activities. A peaceful retreat for couples, families and nature lovers.

Photo Gallery

3-Day Itinerary

Day 1 — Coffee Plantations & Abbey Falls

Explore coffee estates, learn roasting & visit scenic Abbey Falls.

Day 2 — Tala Kaveri & Raja’s Seat

Visit Tala Kaveri Temple (river origin) & sunset viewpoint at Raja’s Seat.

Day 3 — Wildlife & River Rafting

Visit Dubare Elephant Camp or try river rafting depending on season.
