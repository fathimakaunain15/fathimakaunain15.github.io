Canada — Majestic Landscapes & Cities

Snow-capped peaks, turquoise lakes, vibrant cities, wildlife and endless natural beauty — Canada is a dream for every traveler.

Best time: May–Oct (Summer) / Dec–Feb (Snow) Ideal trip: 7–14 days Perfect for: Nature, Adventure, Honeymoon

Quick Overview

Canada is one of the most beautiful countries in the world with iconic destinations like Banff, Vancouver, Toronto, Niagara Falls, Montreal and Whistler. From lakes to mountains to cities — it’s a perfect blend of nature and modern life.

Top Places to Visit

  • Banff National Park — turquoise lakes & mountains
  • Niagara Falls — iconic waterfall experience
  • Toronto — CN Tower, skyline views, nightlife
  • Vancouver — beaches, forests & modern city life
  • Whistler — skiing & snowboarding paradise
  • Montreal — French architecture & culture
  • Quebec City — old town European charm

Day 7 — Montreal

Explore old town, churches & French cafes.
