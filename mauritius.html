Mauritius – Romantic Lagoon & White Sand Honeymoon

Turquoise lagoons, white sand beaches, private pool villas and dreamy sunsets for the perfect couple escape.

Indian Ocean Island Best Time: May – Oct Beaches • Water Villas • Luxury

Why Choose Mauritius for Honeymoon?

Mauritius is a picture-perfect island with calm blue lagoons, luxurious beach resorts, coral reefs and romantic sunsets. It is ideal for couples who want both relaxation and fun water activities like snorkeling, catamaran cruises and underwater walks.

Highlights for Couples

  • Private candlelight dinners on the beach
  • Catamaran sunset cruise with dolphins
  • Snorkeling in crystal-clear lagoons
  • Couple spa & massage sessions
  • Romantic walks on white sand beaches

Best Honeymoon Areas

  • Grand Baie — lively & modern
  • Flic-en-Flac — long sunset beaches
  • Belle Mare — calm and peaceful
  • Le Morne — luxury resorts & views

3-Day Romantic Honeymoon Itinerary

Day 1 — Arrival & Relaxing Beach Day

Check into your resort, decorate your room for honeymoon (many hotels do it), relax by the pool, and enjoy a romantic dinner with sea view at night.

Day 2 — Catamaran Cruise & Water Activities

Go for a full-day catamaran cruise: snorkeling, spotting dolphins, BBQ lunch on board and swimming in turquoise lagoons. Return for a couple spa session in the evening.

Day 3 — South Island & Viewpoints

Visit Chamarel Seven Colored Earth, Black River Gorges viewpoints and Le Morne area. End your trip watching sunset on the beach, capturing honeymoon photos.

Perfect for Honeymoon Couples Who…

  • Love peaceful beaches more than crowded cities.
  • Want an international trip but not too hectic.
  • Enjoy water sports like snorkeling, sailing, kayaking.
  • Prefer all-inclusive luxury resorts and privacy.

How to Reach

  • Fly to Sir Seewoosagur Ramgoolam International Airport.
  • Pre-book an airport transfer with your resort.
  • Visa rules differ by country — check before booking.

Packing Tips for Couples

  • Light cotton clothes, swimsuits & flip-flops.
  • Reef-safe sunscreen, sunglasses & hats.
  • One nice outfit for candlelight dinners.
  • Waterproof phone pouch for boat trips.
  • Basic medicines & travel documents in one pouch.
