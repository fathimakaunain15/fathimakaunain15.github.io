Ooty — Queen of Hill Stations

Tamil Nadu Best Time: Oct–Jun Nature, Lakes, Tea Gardens

Quick Overview

Ooty is famous for misty hills, emerald lakes, lush tea gardens, and cool weather throughout the year. A perfect romantic and family destination located in the Nilgiri hills.

Photo Gallery

3-Day Itinerary

Day 1 — Lakes & Botanical Garden

Visit Ooty Lake, Government Botanical Garden & Tea Factory.

Day 2 — Coonoor & Tea Plantations

Drive to Coonoor — explore Dolphin’s Nose, Lamb’s Rock & Sim’s Park.

Day 3 — Doddabetta Peak & Shopping

Visit the highest peak of Nilgiris, then shop homemade chocolates & oils.
