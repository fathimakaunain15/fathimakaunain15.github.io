Ooty — Queen of the Hills

Misty mountains, tea gardens, lakes, scenic viewpoints and one of India's most beautiful hill stations.

Best time: Oct–Jun Ideal trip: 2–4 days Altitude: 2,240 m

Quick Overview

Ooty, also known as Udhagamandalam, is a charming hill station in Tamil Nadu famous for its green slopes, cool climate, colonial architecture and heritage toy train. Perfect for families, couples, photographers and nature lovers.

Top Places to Visit

  • Ooty Lake (Boating)
  • Doddabetta Peak (Highest point)
  • Botanical Garden
  • Pykara Lake & Waterfalls
  • Ooty Rose Garden
  • Tea Factory & Chocolate Factory
  • Nilgiri Toy Train Ride

3-Day Ooty Itinerary

Day 1 — Tea Estates & Ooty Lake

Visit botanical garden, tea museum, chocolate factory & evening boating.

Day 2 — Doddabetta & Coonoor

Explore Doddabetta Peak, then Coonoor (Lamb's Rock, Dolphin's Nose).

Day 3 — Pykara & Shooting Point

Enjoy Pykara lake, falls & scenic viewpoints before departure.
