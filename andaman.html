Andaman Islands – Complete Travel Guide

The Andaman Islands are one of India’s most beautiful tropical destinations, located in the Bay of Bengal. Known for crystal-clear waters, white sandy beaches, coral reefs, and lush greenery, the Andaman Islands are perfect for travelers seeking peace, adventure, and natural beauty.

Why Visit Andaman Islands?

Andaman is ideal for honeymooners, families, solo travelers, and adventure lovers. The islands offer a perfect balance of relaxation and excitement through beaches, water sports, and historical attractions.

Top Places to Visit in Andaman

  • Havelock Island: Famous for Radhanagar Beach and scuba diving
  • Neil Island: Peaceful beaches and coral reefs
  • Port Blair: Capital city with Cellular Jail
  • Ross Island: Historic ruins from British era

Best Time to Visit Andaman

The best time to visit the Andaman Islands is from October to May. During this period, the weather is pleasant and ideal for beach activities and water sports.

How to Reach Andaman

Andaman can be reached by air and sea. Flights operate from major Indian cities like Chennai, Kolkata, Delhi, and Bangalore to Port Blair. Ships are also available from Chennai, Kolkata, and Visakhapatnam.

Things to Do in Andaman

  • Scuba diving and snorkeling
  • Sea walking
  • Jet skiing and parasailing
  • Island hopping
  • Sunset views at Radhanagar Beach

Budget & Expenses

A 5–6 day trip to Andaman may cost between ₹25,000 to ₹60,000 per person, depending on accommodation, activities, and season.

Travel Tips

  • Carry enough cash as ATMs are limited
  • Book water activities in advance
  • Respect marine life and local rules
  • Avoid plastic to protect the environment

Is Andaman Worth Visiting?

Absolutely. Andaman Islands offer a unique travel experience in India with pristine beaches, calm surroundings, and unforgettable adventures. It is one of the best destinations for those who love nature and the ocean.
