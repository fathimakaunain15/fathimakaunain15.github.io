Jaisalmer — The Golden Desert City

Thar Desert safaris, golden fort views, camel rides & royal sandstone palaces.

Rajasthan Best Time: Oct – Feb Desert Safari • Fort • Havelis

Quick Overview

Jaisalmer is famous for its golden fort, desert dunes, camel rides, and royal heritage havelis. A magical mix of history, culture, and sunsets.

3-Day Itinerary

Day 1 — Jaisalmer Fort & Havelis

Explore the historic Sonar Fort, Patwon Ki Haveli and local markets.

Day 2 — Thar Desert Safari

Camel safari, jeep ride, sunset over dunes & dinner at desert camp.

Day 3 — Gadisar Lake & Shopping

Boating at the lake, then shop for traditional Rajasthani crafts.
