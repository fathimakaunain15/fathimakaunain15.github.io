Auli — India’s Ski Paradise

Snow-capped views, Asia’s longest ropeway, world-class skiing, and silent Himalayan beauty. Auli is winter heaven.

Uttarakhand Best time: Dec–Feb (snow) Famous for: Skiing, Ropeway, Snow Views

Quick Overview

Auli is India’s skiing capital, known for clean powdered slopes, panoramic views of Nanda Devi and a massive ropeway ride from Joshimath. Whether you want skiing, snowboarding, trekking, or peaceful snow views — Auli is perfect.

Top Attractions

  • Auli Ski Slopes
  • Joshimath–Auli Ropeway
  • Gorson Bugyal Trek
  • Artificial Snow Lake
  • View of Nanda Devi Peak

Travel Tips

  • Snowfall is heavy — wear proper winter shoes.
  • Ropeway tickets fill fast — book early.
  • Travel via 4×4 vehicle in peak winter.

3-Day Auli Itinerary

Day 1 — Arrive & Ropeway Ride

Reach Joshimath → take the iconic ropeway to Auli → explore snow lake & viewpoints.

Day 2 — Skiing & Snow Adventure

Full day skiing/snowboarding training. Capture the stunning Himalayas.

Day 3 — Gorson Bugyal Trek

Short easy trek to Gorson Bugyal meadows for surreal views.

Top Hotels

Clifftop Club

Auli Resort

Comfortable stay near slopes.

How to Reach

  • Nearest Airport: Dehradun (Jolly Grant)
  • Nearest Rail: Rishikesh / Haridwar
  • Road: Rishikesh → Joshimath → Ropeway to Auli
