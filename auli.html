Auli — Snow & Skiing Heaven

Powder snow, panoramic Himalayan peaks, ski slopes and one of India’s best winter destinations for snow lovers.

Best time: Dec–Feb (Snow) Ideal trip: 3–5 days Altitude: ~2,800 m

Quick Overview

Auli is a stunning hill station and ski destination in Uttarakhand, known for its snow-clad slopes, oak forests and breathtaking views of Nanda Devi and other Himalayan peaks. It’s perfect for couples, families and adventure travellers looking for real snow.

Top Places & Experiences

  • Auli Ski Slopes – skiing, snowboarding & snow play
  • Auli Ropeway – one of Asia’s longest cable cars with insane views
  • Gorson Bugyal – meadow trek with snow in winter
  • Joshimath – base town with temples & markets
  • Artificial Lake – snow-making reservoir & viewpoint
  • Sunrise & Sunset Points – Himalayan golden hour views

3–4 Day Auli Itinerary

Day 1 — Joshimath Arrival

Reach Joshimath, check into hotel, local market walk, early rest.

Day 2 — Ropeway & Ski Slopes

Take the ropeway to Auli, enjoy snow play & beginner skiing lessons.

Day 3 — Gorson Bugyal Trek

Short trek / hike towards Gorson Bugyal (seasonal), views of Nanda Devi.

Day 4 — Extra Snow Day / Departure

Keep as buffer day for more snow fun or travel back from Joshimath.

How to Reach Auli

  • Nearest Airport: Dehradun (Jolly Grant) – then drive ~9–10 hours to Joshimath.
  • By Train: Nearest major station – Rishikesh / Haridwar, then drive.
  • By Road: Rishikesh → Srinagar → Rudraprayag → Karnaprayag → Joshimath → Auli.
  • Last Stretch: Ropeway or road from Joshimath to Auli (depending on season).
