Why Visit Norway?

Norway is the world’s most magical Northern Lights destination — with Arctic snow, fjords, glass igloos, reindeer, and dramatic mountain landscapes.

Top Highlights

  • Northern Lights viewing (Tromsø & Lofoten)
  • Fjord cruises & Arctic landscapes
  • Dog sledding & snowmobile tours
  • Glass igloo & ice hotel stays
  • Reindeer feeding & Sami culture

3-Day Arctic Itinerary

Day 1 — Tromsø Arrival & Aurora Hunt

Arrive in Tromsø, join a guided Northern Lights chase in a heated bus or snow camp.

Day 2 — Dog Sledding & Fjords

Experience husky dog sledding, explore snowy fjords, and enjoy Arctic food.

Day 3 — Reindeer + Glass Igloo Stay

Meet reindeer, learn Sami culture, then spend a night in a glass igloo under the aurora.
