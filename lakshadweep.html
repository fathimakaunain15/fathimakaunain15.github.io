Why Lakshadweep for Honeymoon?

Lakshadweep is India's most untouched island paradise — crystal lagoons, coral reefs, powdery white sand, and peaceful luxury stays.

Romantic Experiences

  • Private lagoon photoshoot
  • Snorkeling & coral reef diving
  • Island hopping — Bangaram & Thinnakara
  • Romantic sunset cruise
  • Private beach candlelight dinner

Best Islands

  • Agatti — luxury stays & lagoon
  • Bangaram — Maldives feel
  • Minicoy — lighthouse & beaches
  • Kavaratti — coral reefs

3-Day Romantic Honeymoon Itinerary

Day 1 — Agatti Island

Arrive at Agatti. Enjoy lagoon beach, relax at your resort, candlelight dinner by the sea.

Day 2 — Bangaram Island Trip

Visit Bangaram Island: snorkeling, couple photoshoot, sunset cruise, private beach walk.

Day 3 — Minicoy Lighthouse & Lagoon

Explore Minicoy, climb lighthouse, enjoy blue lagoon kayaking, return to Agatti.
