Bali — Tropical Island Paradise

Waterfalls, beaches, rice terraces, sunsets, temples & luxurious resorts — Bali is a magical dream island.

Best time: Apr–Oct Ideal trip: 5–7 days Perfect for: Honeymoon & Adventure

Quick Overview

Bali is Indonesia’s most famous island — known for its tropical beaches, sacred temples, waterfalls, nightlife, cafes, and romantic resorts. It’s perfect for couples, friends, families and solo travellers.

Top Places to Visit

  • Ubud — rice terraces, monkey forest, cafes
  • Nusa Penida — cliffs, blue beaches, photo spots
  • Seminyak — nightlife, beach clubs, shopping
  • Uluwatu — cliff temple & sunsets
  • Mount Batur Sunrise Trek
  • Tegenungan Waterfall

5-Day Bali Itinerary

Day 1 — Ubud

Monkey Forest, waterfalls, cafes, relaxing.

Day 2 — Nusa Penida

Kelingking, Broken Beach, Crystal Bay.

Day 3 — Uluwatu

Uluwatu Temple, cliff views, sunset shows.

Day 4 — Seminyak

Beach clubs, nightlife, shopping.

Day 5 — Mount Batur

Optional sunrise trek & spa relaxation.
