3-Day Itinerary

Why Finland?

Finland, especially Lapland, is the world’s most magical winter land — Northern Lights, glass igloo resorts, frozen forests, ice hotels, reindeer rides and the Arctic Circle.

Top Highlights

  • See Northern Lights from a glass igloo
  • Husky & reindeer safaris
  • Explore the Arctic Circle in Rovaniemi
  • Frozen lakes & snow hiking
  • Visit Santa Claus Village

Photo Gallery

3-Day Best Itinerary

Day 1 — Arrival & Glass Igloo Stay

Arrive in Rovaniemi, stay in a glass igloo resort, watch the night sky for Auroras.

Day 2 — Husky Safari & Arctic Circle

Experience husky sledding, reindeer farm visits and cross the Arctic Circle.

Day 3 — Snow Forests & Ice Activities

Visit Santa Village, snow hiking, frozen lake walks, ice sauna experiences.

Top Stays

Kakslauttanen Arctic Resort

Famous glass igloos with Aurora views.

Arctic TreeHouse Hotel

Luxury cube cabins in the forest.

How to Reach

  • Fly to Helsinki
  • Take a domestic flight to Rovaniemi
  • Snowmobile & tours available everywhere
