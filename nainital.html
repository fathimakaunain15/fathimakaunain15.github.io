Nainital — Lakes & Snow Views

A charming lake town in Uttarakhand with boat rides, snow viewpoints, cozy cafes and peaceful hillside stays — perfect for couples, families and weekend getaways.

Best time: Oct–Feb (snow views) · Mar–Jun Ideal trip: 2–4 days Altitude: ~2,000 m

Quick Overview

Nainital is built around the beautiful, pear-shaped Naini Lake and surrounded by forested hills. It offers boating, ropeway rides, viewpoints, cafés and easy access to snow in winter at nearby higher points.

Top Attractions

  • Naini Lake & Mall Road
  • Naina Devi Temple
  • Snow View Point (ropeway)
  • Tiffin Top / Dorothy's Seat
  • Eco Cave Gardens
  • Hanuman Garhi sunset point

Travel Tips

  • Carry warm layers, especially from Oct–Feb.
  • Book lake-facing hotels early on weekends & holidays.
  • Walking shoes recommended for steep slopes & viewpoints.
  • During peak season, start sightseeing early to avoid traffic.

3-Day Nainital Itinerary

Day 1 — Lake & Mall Road

Arrive in Nainital, check in to your hotel and head for an evening boat ride on Naini Lake followed by a stroll and dinner at Mall Road.

Day 2 — Snow View & Tiffin Top

Take the ropeway to Snow View Point for Himalayan vistas, then trek/ride to Tiffin Top or visit Eco Cave Gardens in the evening.

Day 3 — Temples & Sunset

Visit Naina Devi Temple in the morning, explore local markets and end the day with a sunset at Hanuman Garhi before departure.

Extra day? Add a side trip to Bhimtal, Sattal or Naukuchiatal for quieter lakes and nature.

How to Reach Nainital

  • Nearest Rail: Kathgodam Railway Station (~35 km).
  • By Road: Good road connectivity from Delhi, Bareilly, Haldwani, Bhimtal, etc.
  • From Delhi: Approx. 7–8 hours by car or bus (via Hapur–Moradabad–Haldwani).
  • By Air: Nearest airport is Pantnagar (~70 km), limited flights.
