Why New Zealand?

New Zealand is pure cinematic beauty — mountains, blue lakes, fjords, glaciers, alpines, and the best adventure activities in the world. Queenstown is known as the “Adventure Capital”.

Top Highlights

  • Milford Sound Fjords — most beautiful place in NZ
  • Queenstown — adventure capital
  • Wanaka Lake views
  • Helicopter glacier flights
  • Jet boating & bungee jumping

3-Day Best Itinerary

Day 1 — Queenstown Adventure

Enjoy jet boating, gondola rides, bungee jumping and lakeside views.

Day 2 — Milford Sound

Take a scenic fjord cruise, waterfalls, wildlife & mountains everywhere.

Day 3 — Wanaka & Blue Lake Views

Visit Lake Wanaka, Roy’s Peak view, helicopter glacier tours optional.

Top Stays

St Moritz Queenstown
