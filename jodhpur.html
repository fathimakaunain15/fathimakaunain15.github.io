Jodhpur — The Royal Blue City

Mehrangarh Fort, blue houses, palaces, lakes & royal Rajasthani culture.

Rajasthan Best Time: Oct – Feb Blue Streets • Fort • Palaces

Quick Overview

Jodhpur, known as the Blue City, is famous for its massive fort, royal palaces, blue-painted houses, lakes and incredible sunset views.

Photo Gallery

3-Day Itinerary

Day 1 — Mehrangarh Fort & Blue Streets

Explore the massive fort, museum & walk through the blue-painted lanes.

Day 2 — Umaid Bhawan Palace & Jaswant Thada

Visit the royal palace & the beautiful marble cenotaph.

Day 3 — Toorji Ka Jhalra Stepwell & Clock Tower

Relax near the stepwell & shop around the old market streets.
