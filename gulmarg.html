Gulmarg — Snow Meadows & Gondola Heights

World's highest gondola, perfect skiing slopes, pine forests and white landscapes — Gulmarg is winter heaven.

Jammu & Kashmir Best time: Dec–March (snow) Famous for: Gondola, Skiing, Snow Fields

Quick Overview

Gulmarg is one of the best winter destinations in Asia. The Gulmarg Gondola lifts you to 13,000+ ft, offering surreal views and world-class skiing slopes. The silent pine forests and snow fields feel unreal in winter.

Top Attractions

  • Gulmarg Gondola (Phase 1 & 2)
  • Strawberry Valley
  • Departure point for skiing
  • Children’s Park & snow play
  • Apharwat Peak

Travel Tips

  • Ski gear available for rent.
  • Book Gondola tickets early.
  • Walking in snow—carry snow boots.

3-Day Gulmarg Itinerary

Day 1 — Arrive & Explore Snow Fields

Walk around the meadows, explore snow areas and enjoy photography among pine forests.

Day 2 — Gondola Phase 1 & 2

Ride the famous Gondola up to Apharwat Peak. Surreal snow views at 13,400 ft.

Day 3 — Skiing / Snowboarding

Take skiing lessons and enjoy snow sports with trained instructors.

Top Hotels

Khyber Resort

Luxury stay with pine forest views.

Hotel Highlands

Comfortable & affordable stay.

How to Reach

  • Nearest Airport: Srinagar
  • Nearest Rail: Jammu Tawi
  • Srinagar → Gulmarg: 1.5–2 hours
