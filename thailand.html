Thailand — Islands, Temples & Night Markets

Bangkok temples, Phuket & Krabi beaches, island hopping, street food and vibrant nightlife.

Southeast Asia Best Time: Nov – Feb Beaches • Temples • Night Markets

Quick Overview

Thailand blends tropical islands and buzzing cities. From Bangkok’s temples and markets to Phuket/Krabi’s limestone cliffs and Phi Phi’s blue waters — it’s ideal for beach lovers, foodies and culture explorers.

3-Day Suggested Itinerary (Bangkok + Beach)

Day 1 — Bangkok: Temples & River

Visit Grand Palace, Wat Pho, take a Chao Phraya river cruise; evening at Khao San Road or a rooftop bar.

Day 2 — Fly to Phuket / Krabi: Beach Time

Morning flight to Phuket or Krabi. Relax on the beach, try local seafood, sunset view at Promthep Cape (Phuket) or Railay Beach (Krabi).

Day 3 — Island Hopping (Phi Phi / James Bond Island)

Boat trip to Phi Phi islands or Phang Nga Bay (James Bond Island), snorkeling and lagoon visits. Return in evening.

Top Experiences

  • Street food tour — try pad thai, mango sticky rice, tom yum
  • Night markets — Chiang Mai, Phuket weekend markets, Bangkok night markets
  • Snorkeling/diving in Phi Phi & Similan Islands
  • Visit Wat Arun & Wat Pho, see the reclining Buddha
  • Thai massage & spa day

Packing & Safety Tips

  • Light breathable clothes; bring a light rain jacket in monsoon months.
  • Respect temples: cover shoulders & knees; remove shoes inside.
  • Drink bottled water; be cautious with street food if you have a sensitive stomach.
  • Use licensed taxis or Grab app in cities; keep valuables secure on beaches.
