Maldives Travel Guide (2026) – 5 Day Itinerary, Budget & Resort Tips

The Maldives is one of the most beautiful island destinations in the world. With turquoise waters, overwater villas and coral reefs, it is a dream destination for honeymooners and beach lovers.

This detailed guide covers a realistic 5-day Maldives plan, budget estimate for Indian travelers, where to stay, and important tips before booking.

Why Visit Maldives?

Maldives offers peaceful beaches, crystal-clear lagoons and luxury island experiences. It’s perfect for couples, anniversary trips and relaxation holidays.

Best Time to Visit Maldives

  • November – April: Best season (dry weather)
  • May – October: Lower prices but possible rain

5-Day Maldives Itinerary

Day 1 – Arrival & Resort Check-in

Transfer by speedboat or seaplane to your island resort. Relax and enjoy sunset views.

Day 2 – Water Activities

Snorkeling, kayaking and swimming in clear lagoons. Optional scuba diving.

Day 3 – Island Exploration

Visit nearby local island or enjoy spa treatment. Relax by private beach.

Day 4 – Adventure & Photography

Sunset cruise, dolphin watching and underwater photography.

Day 5 – Leisure & Departure

Morning beach walk and return to Malé airport.

Maldives Budget (Per Person – 5 Days)

  • Flights: ₹20,000 – ₹35,000
  • Resort Stay: ₹40,000 – ₹1,20,000
  • Food (if not included): ₹8,000 – ₹15,000
  • Activities: ₹10,000 – ₹20,000

Total Estimated Budget: ₹80,000 – ₹1,80,000

Where to Stay

  • Budget: Local islands like Maafushi
  • Mid-range: Standard beach resorts
  • Luxury: Overwater villa resorts

What to Eat

  • Grilled seafood
  • Tuna dishes
  • Coconut curries
  • Tropical fruits

Common Travel Mistakes

  • Ignoring transfer costs
  • Not checking meal inclusions
  • Booking without comparing resort reviews
  • Underestimating water activity prices

FAQs

Is Maldives expensive?
Yes, but budget options are available on local islands.

Is visa required?
No pre-visa required for Indians. Visa on arrival is available.

How many days are enough?
4–5 days are ideal for a relaxing trip.

Final Thoughts

Maldives is perfect for those seeking luxury and tranquility. With proper planning, it can be a once-in-a-lifetime experience.
