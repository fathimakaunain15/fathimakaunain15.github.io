Mussoorie — Queen of the Hills

Cloudy weather, ropeway views, waterfalls & romantic mountain scenery.

Uttarakhand Best Time: March – June / Sept – Dec Clouds • Ropeway • Waterfalls

Quick Overview

Mussoorie is a beautiful hill station known for cloudy mornings, mountain viewpoints, ropeways, shopping streets and refreshing cold air. A perfect romantic getaway.

3-Day Itinerary

Day 1 — Kempty Falls & Mall Road

Visit the famous waterfall & enjoy shopping and cafes.

Day 2 — Gun Hill Ropeway

Take a ropeway ride to the mountain top for breathtaking views.

Day 3 — Camel’s Back Road Walk

Relax with a peaceful nature walk overlooking the mountains.
