Iceland — Glaciers, Waterfalls & Northern Lights

Black beaches, volcanoes, ice caves, waterfalls, hot springs & magical auroras.

Quick Overview

Iceland is one of the most unique destinations on Earth — with dramatic landscapes, roaring waterfalls, ice caves, volcanoes, geothermal pools & stunning Northern Lights.

Top Attractions

  • Blue Lagoon — geothermal spa
  • Reynisfjara Black Sand Beach
  • Gullfoss & Skogafoss waterfalls
  • Diamond Beach (ice blocks on shore)
  • Vatnajökull Ice Caves
  • Northern Lights (Sept–March)
