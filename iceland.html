Why Iceland?

A land where glaciers meet volcanoes, with surreal landscapes — blue geothermal pools, lava fields, powerful waterfalls and black-sand beaches.

Top Highlights

  • Blue Lagoon — thermal spa
  • Golden Circle — geysers & waterfalls
  • Black sand beaches — Reynisfjara
  • Volcano & glacier hikes
  • Northern Lights (Sept–March)

3-Day Best Itinerary

Day 1 — Blue Lagoon & Reykjavik

Relax in the famous geothermal spa, explore Reykjavik city & try Icelandic cuisine.

Day 2 — Golden Circle

Visit Þingvellir, Geysir, Gullfoss & volcanic landscapes with glacier views.

Day 3 — Black-Sand Beaches & Waterfalls

See waterfalls like Skogafoss + Reynisfjara black-sand beach + basalt cliffs.
