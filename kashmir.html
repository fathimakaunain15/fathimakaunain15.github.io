Kashmir — Paradise on Earth

Snow peaks, shikara rides, Mughal gardens, pine forests, meadows & winter magic — Kashmir is India's most beautiful travel destination.

Best time: Oct–Feb (Snow) · Apr–Jun Ideal trip: 5–7 days Altitude: 1,585 m to 2,700 m

Quick Overview

Kashmir offers breathtaking valleys, floating markets, snowy regions, scenic meadows and iconic houseboats. It is perfect for honeymoons, family vacations and photography lovers.

Top Places to Visit

  • Gulmarg — Skiing, snow & gondola ride
  • Sonmarg — Meadows & glaciers
  • Pahalgam — River valleys & pine forests
  • Srinagar — Dal Lake, gardens, houseboats
  • Betaab Valley — Famous Bollywood valley
  • Aru Valley — Horse rides & hills

5-Day Kashmir Itinerary

Day 1 — Srinagar & Dal Lake

Shikara ride, floating market & Mughal gardens.

Day 2 — Gulmarg

Gondola Phase 1 & 2, skiing, snow activities.

Day 3 — Sonmarg

Zero Point & Thajiwas Glacier (horse rides).

Day 4 — Pahalgam

Aru Valley, Betaab Valley, Chandanwari.

Day 5 — Srinagar Local

Hazratbal, markets, café hopping, shopping.
