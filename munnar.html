Munnar — Tea Gardens, Mist & Valleys

Rolling tea estates, misty mountains, waterfalls and romantic views.

Kerala Best Time: October – April Tea Estates • Waterfalls • Viewpoints

Quick Overview

Munnar is Kerala’s most famous hill station known for endless green tea plantations, mist-covered valleys, waterfalls and cool weather. Perfect for couples, families and nature lovers.

3-Day Itinerary

Day 1 — Tea Gardens & Photo Point

Visit sprawling tea estates, Photo Point and Tea Museum.

Day 2 — Eravikulam National Park

See Nilgiri Tahr, scenic views & visit Rajamalai hills.

Day 3 — Waterfalls & Dam

Explore Attukal Waterfalls & Mattupetty Dam boating.
