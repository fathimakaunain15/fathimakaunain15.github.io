Paris Travel Guide (2026) – 5 Day Itinerary, Budget & Planning Tips

Paris, the capital of France, is one of the most iconic cities in the world. Known as the “City of Love,” it is famous for its historic landmarks, romantic streets, art museums and world-class cuisine.

This guide will help you plan a practical 5-day Paris trip, including a realistic budget from India, daily itinerary and helpful travel tips.

Why Visit Paris?

Paris offers a perfect mix of history, art, architecture and food. From the Eiffel Tower to the Louvre Museum, every corner of the city feels elegant and timeless.

Best Time to Visit Paris

  • April – June: Pleasant weather & spring bloom
  • September – October: Mild weather & fewer crowds
  • December: Festive Christmas atmosphere

5-Day Paris Itinerary

Day 1 – Eiffel Tower & Seine River

Visit Eiffel Tower observation deck and take evening Seine river cruise.

Day 2 – Louvre & Notre Dame

Explore Louvre Museum and see Mona Lisa. Visit Notre Dame Cathedral area.

Day 3 – Montmartre

Visit Sacré-Cœur Basilica and explore artistic streets.

Day 4 – Versailles

Day trip to Palace of Versailles for royal experience.

Day 5 – Shopping & Cafés

Explore Champs-Élysées and enjoy Parisian café culture.

Paris Budget (Per Person – 5 Days)

  • Flights: ₹40,000 – ₹70,000
  • Stay: ₹35,000 – ₹70,000
  • Transport Pass: ₹6,000
  • Food: ₹20,000 – ₹30,000
  • Attractions: ₹15,000 – ₹25,000

Total Estimated Budget: ₹1,20,000 – ₹2,00,000

Where to Stay

  • Budget: Hostels near city center
  • Mid-range: 3-star hotels near Opera district
  • Luxury: Eiffel Tower view hotels

What to Eat in Paris

  • Croissants
  • Baguettes
  • Macarons
  • French cheese & wine

Common Travel Mistakes

  • Not booking museum tickets in advance
  • Ignoring metro transport pass
  • Visiting only tourist spots without exploring neighborhoods
  • Underestimating walking distances

FAQs

Is Paris expensive?
Yes, but smart budgeting can manage costs.

Is Schengen visa required?
Yes, Indian travelers need Schengen visa.

How many days are enough?
4–5 days are ideal.

Final Thoughts

Paris is timeless and unforgettable. With proper planning, it offers a beautiful mix of romance, history and cultural richness.
