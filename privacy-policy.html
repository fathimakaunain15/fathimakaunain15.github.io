Privacy Policy

At AdventureHub, accessible from https://fathimakaunain15.github.io/, the privacy of our visitors is one of our main priorities. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that are collected and recorded by AdventureHub and how we use it.

Log Files

AdventureHub follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected by log files include internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks.

Cookies and Web Beacons

Like any other website, AdventureHub uses ‘cookies’. These cookies are used to store information including visitors’ preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited.

Google AdSense

Google is one of a third-party vendor on our site. It uses cookies, known as DART cookies, to serve ads to our site visitors based upon their visit to www.website.com and other sites
